I am thrilled to express my enthusiastic interest in the "Assistant Data Engineer" position at Sun Hung Kai Properties. As a dedicated researcher and accomplished software developer with a strong foundation in data-oriented challenges, I am eager to leverage my skills and expertise in a practical business setting through this role.

My journey began in the realm of physics, where I conducted rigorous data analytical research on complex astronomical data. Through this experience, I honed my skills in C, Python, Linux systems, supercomputing, and simulations. Building upon this foundation, my PhD research took an innovative turn as I delved into computational social science, conducting projects such as the analysis of gender biases in academic funding application systems. This endeavor equipped me with robust proficiency in Python, pandas, SQL, and databases. To continually push my boundaries, I dedicated substantial effort to crafting a sophisticated simulation framework and a game using Kotlin, a language closely related to Java. These undertakings underscore my adaptability to diverse object-oriented languages and frameworks.

In parallel with my academic pursuits, I actively contributed to open-source projects, elevating my abilities from a scientific programmer to a versatile software engineer. This evolution underscores my unwavering commitment to excellence in software development and my adeptness at embracing new challenges.

Sun Hung Kai Properties stands out to me not only for its impressive projects but also for its commitment to innovation and collaboration. I am particularly drawn to the opportunity to contribute to your data engineering team and drive impactful solutions.

I eagerly anticipate the prospect of joining your team and making a meaningful contribution. Thank you for considering my application.
